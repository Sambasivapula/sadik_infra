completed until that point was aligned with the planned specifications.   
Impact:  
This initiative significantly contributed to improving living conditions for underprivileged 
families by provid ing affordable and quality housing.

The pipes were placed in the excavated trenches using hydra cranes , and the ends of the pipes 
were joined  during placement. The total volume of work completed was over 1.5 lakh cubic 
meters . The actual value of the work , as agreed by the government, w as ₹5,37,79,500. 
Challenges and Delays : 
The project faced significant interruptions due to the 2019 elections , leading to a temporary 
halt in work when the ruling party changed. The work remained on hold until the next 
elections , as the project was directly linked to the continuity of the same political party . In 
2024, with the same party returning to power, work is gradually resuming and progressing.  
Impact : 
This water sewage system plays a crucial role in providing water to th e newly developed capital 
of Amaravathi , contributing to the infrastructure development of the state.
